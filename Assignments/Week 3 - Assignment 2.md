## Meta & Objectives

### AI Image Prompt Used for Connective Visuals

**Prompt for hero texture / background:**

> Create a minimal abstract technical background for a cybersecurity and AI research portfolio. Use a calm near-black and blue palette with small green signal accents. The image should feel like a subtle network graph, terminal grid, or research map, but it must stay minimal and not look like a dramatic hacker poster. No text, no fake code, no fake dashboards, no people. Clean, professional, quiet, precise.

**Prompt for small icons:**

> Create a consistent set of minimal line icons for a technical portfolio. Icons needed: AI systems, cybersecurity, backend engineering, research notes. Use simple geometric strokes, rounded corners, and a calm technical mood. No gradients, no complex details, no text, no fake logos. The icons should work at small sizes and match a minimal blue/green portfolio identity.
